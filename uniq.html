UniQ

A powerful tool for identification of students in risk of dropout

UniQ is based on machine learning and uses the master's data that educational institutions have on their students. Based on large amounts of data, patterns are identified by former students and used for risk identification on present students.

UniQ perform an early identification of the risk, and the reason for, a change in the student performance during its study.

UniQ identify who is at risk of terminating the study within a limited time period and why. This gives the institution staff a unique opportunity to meet the student in a timely manner and help them to maintain its study.

Overall, our customers will have a lower drop-out rate and students will receive help on time, before a possible dropout, and prospectively experience a better study environment.

StruX

A powerful tool for organizational analysis and simulations

StruX has been developed and designed as an HR and senior management tool to support both short and long-term organizational decisions in terms of political and strategic tasks.

StruX is an algorithm based analysis tool that assesses the ability to manor a given current and future structure in an organization.

StruX describes the necessary initiatives needed to staff a desired structure, and thereby the expected costs for a desired future condition.

For example, if your company wants to grow and this growth requires, an increased staffing, this tool is designed to describe the consequences of those conditions.

StruX also describes whether the present and future organizational structure will be able to sustaine itself and its consequences.

  • Applications

  • The current and future staffing capacity of the organization

  • Organization's current and future sustainability.

  • Optimal access in relation to sustainability.

  • Cost of staffing growth.

  • Recognition of limiting factors for development.

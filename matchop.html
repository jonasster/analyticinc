MatchOp

A powerful tool for optimization of sales processes

MatchOp is a software program designed to optimize the sales process for companies. MatchOp finds and predicts the optimal match between a lead, subject or customer and a salesman, broker or product.

MatchOp optimizes the sales process based on the data already in the organization, as well as data from open databases in Denmark and Europe.

MatchOp is based on machine learning and is an online algorithm that is constantly becoming wiser and smarter on your customers and continually optimizes the sales process.

MatchOp does not export data from your organization at any time and therefore does not conflict with GDPR, all data and results of analyzes will stay in your organization.

  • Other uses

  • MatchOp also has applications elsewhere. In general, MatchOp can be used where there is a desire to select a particular segment of a group of individuals.

    In the sales process, MatchOp examines where an individual fits best into a sales organization. It is possible to reverse the analysis and investigate how well a person fits into an organization or to a product.

    MatchOp can also be used to assess how long a person stays a customer, how long an employee is considered to stays with the company.
